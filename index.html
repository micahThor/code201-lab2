My Bio

My name is Micah and I grew up in a small agricultural town called Mims, Florida. After graduating high school I joined the Air Force as an avionics technician. Between training stints and deployments I got to call many places home. After the military I continued my career at Bombardier Aerospace, and took on Army contracts for a small aircraft modification company. Within the past few years I moved to Seattle to transition careers into the IT sector, and recently I was hired at Amazon to participate in their software developer engineer apprentice program.

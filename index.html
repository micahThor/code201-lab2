Code 201 Week 1 - Micah Thorstenson's About Me

My Bio

My name is Micah and I grew up in a small town called Mims, Florida. After graduating high school I joined the Air Force as an avionics technician. Between training stints and deployments I got to call many places home. When my enlistment ended I continued my career as an aircraft technician at Bombardier Aerospace, and took on Army contracts for a small aircraft modification company. Within the past few years I moved to Seattle to transition careers. I recently graduated from North Seattle College with an associates degree, and was hired at Amazon to participate in their software developer engineer apprentice program.
